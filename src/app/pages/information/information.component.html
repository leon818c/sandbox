Become an Altar Server

Join our community of dedicated young people serving at St. Alphonsa Parish

What is Altar Serving?

Altar servers assist the priest during Mass and other liturgical celebrations. It's a special way to participate more fully in the worship of our Church and grow closer to God.

Requirements

  • Must be in 4th grade or older
  • Have received First Holy Communion
  • Willing to attend training sessions
  • Committed to serving regularly

What You'll Learn

  • Proper liturgical procedures
  • How to assist during Mass
  • Respect for sacred objects
  • Leadership and responsibility

Get Started

Ready to join? Contact the parish office or speak with Father after Mass. Training sessions are held monthly for new altar servers.
